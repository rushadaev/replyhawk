FAQ

Common questions.

Don't see yours? Email us — we reply faster than our bot.

Is this safe for my Yelp / Thumbtack account?

Yes. You sign into Yelp and Thumbtack inside the ReplyHawk app, exactly as you normally would in your browser. Your login stays encrypted on your own Mac — we never see or store your password, and everything runs from your own machine and IP, so it looks like you using your own account (because it is).

How does the AI know what to say?

You set up a short business profile in the dashboard: services, prices, hours, license, tone, services-not-offered. The AI uses that profile, plus the full conversation history, to write each reply in your voice. You can also paste in a few of your favorite past replies as voice examples.

What if the AI gets a question it can't handle?

It collects the customer's contact info, sends you an SMS and email immediately, and steps aside so you can pick up. You can also jump in at any time — the bot detects when you start typing and yields the conversation.

How fast is setup?

About two minutes. Install the Mac app, sign into your Yelp Business and Thumbtack Pro accounts once, and turn it on. Free setup help if you want it.

Can the AI actually call my customer and talk?

Yes. On the Pro plan, the optional voice agent answers inbound calls when you can't, and places outbound qualification calls. It can answer in English or Spanish, book the appointment, and write the outcome back to your inbox.

Can I cancel anytime?

Anytime, in two clicks. No contracts. If you cancel mid-month, you only pay for leads we already replied to.
