FAQ

Common questions.

Don't see yours? Email us — we reply faster than our bot.

Is this safe for my Yelp / Thumbtack account?

Yes. You sign into Yelp and Thumbtack inside the ReplyHawk app, exactly as you normally would in your browser. Your login stays encrypted on your own Mac — we never see or store your password, and everything runs from your own machine and IP, so it looks like you using your own account (because it is).

How does the AI know what to say?

You fill in a short business profile in the dashboard — services, hours, tone, and jobs you don't take — plus any specific reply instructions. The AI uses that, along with the full conversation, to write each reply in your voice. It never quotes a price; it says a tech confirms after seeing the job.

What if the AI gets a question it can't handle?

Once a job is booked the AI stops replying on its own — and if that customer messages again, the lead is flagged with a notification so a person can take it. You can also open any conversation and reply yourself at any time.

What do I actually see in the dashboard?

One board with every lead from every platform, sorted into New → Contacted → Engaged → Booked → Closed. Each card shows the next scheduled action — initial call/reply, a follow-up with a countdown, or "needs a human." Open a lead for the full conversation, call transcripts, and activity log.

Can I upload my own contact list?

Yes. Paste a list of names and numbers and each becomes a lead. With the pipeline on, the voice agent calls through them automatically. Only upload contacts you have permission to call.

How fast is setup?

A few minutes. Install the Mac app, sign into your Yelp Business and Thumbtack Pro accounts once, and turn it on. Free setup help if you want it.

Can the AI actually call my customer and talk?

Yes. The voice agent places the call, qualifies the job, and books the appointment — in English or Spanish — then writes the full transcript and outcome back to the lead.

Can I cancel anytime?

Anytime. No contracts. If you cancel mid-month, you only pay for the leads we already contacted.
