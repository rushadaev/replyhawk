# ReplyHawk — Brand Guide

## Name
**ReplyHawk** — *one word, capital R, capital H.*
- Domain target: `replyhawk.ai` (primary), `replyhawk.com` (redirect)
- Email: `partnerships@replyhawk.ai`, `founders@replyhawk.ai`

## Tagline
**"Reply first. Win every lead."**

Alt: "AI that replies in 10 seconds — for every Yelp, Thumbtack, and Google lead."

## One-line description
ReplyHawk is an AI lead-response platform for home-service businesses. We auto-reply to Yelp, Thumbtack, and Google LSA inquiries in under 30 seconds, qualify the lead with conversational AI, and call the customer back to book the job — all before competitors see the notification.

## Voice
- Confident, blunt, builder-energy. Talks like a contractor's smartest friend.
- "Win the deal," "book the job," "first to reply wins" — not "engagement" or "conversion funnel."
- Short sentences. Numbers over adjectives. ("10 seconds" beats "lightning fast.")
- Never says "leverage," "synergy," "AI-powered solutions." Will say "the AI handles it" or "we reply for you."

## Visual identity

### Logo
Simple geometric hawk silhouette — sharp triangular head pointing right (forward motion).
Use the inline SVG in `index.html` as the canonical version. Monochrome on light bg, accent-color on dark bg.

### Color palette
| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `--ink`   | `#0B1220` | Hero background, body text on light |
| `--bone`  | `#F7F8FA` | Page background |
| `--card`  | `#FFFFFF` | Cards on light bg |
| `--accent`| `#FF5A1F` | CTAs, hawk eye, hover states (the "strike" color) |
| `--gold`  | `#FFB300` | Secondary accent — sparingly, for icons |
| `--muted` | `#6B7280` | Subhead text |
| `--line`  | `#E5E7EB` | Dividers, card borders |
| `--good`  | `#10B981` | Success states, "live" indicators |

### Typography
- Headings: **Inter** 700 (or system `-apple-system` fallback)
- Body: **Inter** 400/500
- Numbers / stats: **Inter** 800, slightly tighter letter-spacing
- Monospace (for code or response times): `ui-monospace, "SF Mono", monospace`

### Photography
None for v1. Use SVG illustrations and big numbers. Stock photos of "smiling contractor with iPad" cheapen the brand.

## Positioning (one-pager elevator)

> Service businesses on Yelp, Thumbtack, and Google LSA lose 50%+ of leads to whoever replies first.
> The window is 5 minutes. Most pros don't make it.
> ReplyHawk is an AI that replies in under 30 seconds — message, SMS, and an instant call to your phone with the customer on the line.
> Your competitors are still reading the notification when you're already on the call.

## Sales angles to lead with
1. **Speed.** 10–30 second response time, every lead, 24/7.
2. **Trust.** Replies sound like the owner — not a bot. The AI knows the business's services, pricing, license, hours.
3. **End-to-end.** Doesn't just message — places the call too. AI talks to the customer or hands off live to the owner.
4. **One inbox.** Yelp + Thumbtack + Google LSA + SMS in one place. No app-switching.

## Pricing narrative
- $99 setup (or free for first 10 customers)
- $2/lead replied — billed monthly
- First 7 days free
- No contract, cancel anytime

(LeadWinner is $2.99/lead. Undercut by ~30% to win switchers.)

## Founding story (for partnerships emails)

> ReplyHawk was started by a team that watched too many service businesses lose hot leads because they were on a job site. We built the system we wished existed: an AI that replies the moment a Yelp or Thumbtack notification arrives, in the owner's voice, and instantly bridges a call. Our launch customers are contractors and home-service pros across California.

## Do / don't

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Say "AI replies for you" | Say "AI-powered conversational solutions" |
| Say "win the lead" | Say "improve engagement" |
| Lead with the 10-second number | Lead with the tech stack |
| Compare to LeadWinner if asked | Mention LeadWinner first, unprompted |
| Show real screenshots | Use stock photos |
